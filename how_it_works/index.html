How it works

Compare the weather at different mountains on your pass. First, select your pass in the top right-hand corner. Then:

  1. Select a region of the country.
  2. Select a mountain from those in your chosen region.
  3. Click 'Go'.
  4. Repeat for the other mountains you want to compare. You can compare up to 5 mountains.

That's it! You can also click the icon to open that mountain's snow report for today.
